Fictional premises
-----------------------------------------------

.. image:: /_static/images/fictional-premises.png
   :alt: illustration on aged parchment, looking out over the city of Ankh-Morpork from high atop a defensive tower. In the mid-ground, the city and the vast Circle Sea are rendered in detailed, hand-drawn cartography. In the immediate foreground, the stone ramparts of the tower (the 'MoD/Establishment') are visible. Integrated into the foreground view, the different functions are symbolized: a heavy brass cryptographic device for golem computing, a stack of freshly audited ledgers, and a massive, telescope-like magical looking-glass used for long-range observation over the sea.

A concrete geopolitical ecosystem (MoD, Secret Service, White Hat Org, Beastitarian Spy NGO, and NATO parallel),
dressed in other clothes so they can be looked at squarely: a setting for strategic threat modelling, and a ramp
from lore into real-world fire.
