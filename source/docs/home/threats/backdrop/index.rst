The threat backdrop
========================================================================================

Attacks against home users come from a wider range of actors and techniques than most people expect. Nation-states,
organised crime, and opportunistic individuals all operate in the same space, often using the same toolkits. The
techniques themselves are not exotic: credential reuse, social engineering, unpatched software, and devices that
connect to cloud infrastructure without any meaningful security controls.

Understanding what is out there is more useful than a list of rules, because rules age and the underlying patterns
do not change as quickly.
