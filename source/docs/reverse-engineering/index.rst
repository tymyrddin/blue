Reverse engineering
===================================

Understanding how malware is built and how binaries work is prerequisite knowledge for anyone doing incident response,
threat hunting, or detection engineering. You cannot write good detections for behaviour you do not understand, and you
cannot triage an unknown sample without knowing what you are looking at.
