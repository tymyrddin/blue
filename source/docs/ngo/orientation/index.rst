Reading the Signs
==================

.. image:: /_static/images/ngo-orientation.png
   :alt: The entrance hall of the Home on your first morning. You are holding a notebook. The noticeboard ahead is dense with pinned papers — feeding rotas, health and safety reminders, a laminated sign reading DO NOT USE THE PRINTER ON TUESDAYS (no explanation). Through a door to the left, a server room. Through a door to the right, something large and luminescent is asleep. On the floor near your feet, someone has chalked an arrow pointing further in, labelled START HERE. Mrs. Clodpull has her back to you, already walking away.

You have the job description. You have not yet been given the keys. Before you touch
anything, you read what the environment is already telling you: the scale, the signals,
the likely shape of the infrastructure, and the gaps in what you know.
