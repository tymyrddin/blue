Golem Trust Computing Ltd.
=====================================

.. image:: /_static/images/golem-trust-computing.png
   :alt: A ramshackle converted warehouse near the Shades in Ankh Morpork, fog rising from cobbled streets, crooked gas lamps glowing. A hand-painted sign reads Golem Trust Computing.

Golem Trust Computing operates from a converted fish warehouse near the Shades in Ankh-Morpork. Ponder Stibbons
(former HEX operator at Unseen University) and Adora Belle Dearheart (postal systems expert) provide cloud hosting
for the city's merchant guilds.

They host critical systems for the Patrician's Office, the Royal Bank of Ankh-Morpork, and merchant guilds across
the Circle Sea. All infrastructure runs on Hetzner Cloud in Finland. Everything is European
or open source.
