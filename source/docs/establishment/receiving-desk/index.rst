Receiving Desk
=====================================

Coordinated vulnerability disclosure intake for the Civic Defence Establishment. Three channels:
security.txt for standard submissions, PGP-encrypted email for sensitive identified submissions, and
a Tor onion service for anonymous material whose provenance is not recorded.
