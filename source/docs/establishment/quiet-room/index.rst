Quiet Room
=====================================

Signal intake and confidence classification for the Civic Defence Establishment. Suricata and Zeek sensor
outputs, selective Wazuh ingestion, and a classification scheme that auto-scores incoming material by source
taxonomy, with analyst review required before anything is escalated or routed above threshold. The Quiet
Room characterises. It does not interpret.
