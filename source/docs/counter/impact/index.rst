Limiting the blast radius
=========================

Impact is the phase where the attacker makes themselves known, whether they
intended to or not. Ransomware encrypts, wipers delete, botnets recruit, and
service disruptions cascade through dependencies in ways that are difficult to
anticipate. In some intrusions, impact is the goal from the start; in others,
the attacker is forced to act before they are ready. By this point the attacker
has already won the access battle. A defender's job shifts from prevention to
containment, limiting how much damage is done before the environment can be
restored.
