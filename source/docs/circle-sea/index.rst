The Circle Sea Arrangement
==========================

The standing defence understanding that ties the powers around the Circle Sea together: binding,
unnamed, and owned by none of them. Ankh-Morpork sits at its centre, less by command than by being
the city everyone's clacks, coin, and cargo already pass through. These pages set out what the
arrangement is, who is in it, who sits outside, and where the pressure that drives a crisis tends
to come from.
