Analysis
======================================================

A single crime is simple; a conspiracy is the real threat. This stage teaches seeing the threads connecting
separate incidents, moving from barking at footpads to uncovering a Fools' Guild plot. You can learn correlation and
frequency rules to chain alerts across the city, map them to the grand tactics of MITRE, and grasp the critical
difference between a digital street scuffle (the data plane) and a silent coup in the Guild Registry (kept in the
Patrician's Palace, the Guild control plane).
