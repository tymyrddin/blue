Tool proficiency
======================================================

Weapons and warrants: the specific incantations of the Wazuh Rule. Become a clerk of chaos, writing decoders to force
sense from the gibberish of a Sysmon report, crafting precise `<rule>` declarations to define a crime, and using
`<if_sid>` to link related misdeeds. This is the granular craft of turning a single, strange event at the Mended Drum
into a formal alert for the attention of the Dept of Silent Stability.
