Infrastructure
=====================================

The infrastructure Ankh-Morpork's secret service runs, set out one layer at a time: what is
collected, how it moves, where it is held, who may reach it, and who the whole arrangement answers
to. The pages note where the city's version is borrowed, narrowed, or smaller than the work it
carries.
