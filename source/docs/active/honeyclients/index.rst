Honeyclients
=============================================================

Honeyclients take the active role: rather than waiting for attackers to connect, they visit potentially
malicious URLs and observe what happens. They simulate vulnerable browsers to attract exploit kits and
malicious scripts, collecting what traditional honeypots miss.
